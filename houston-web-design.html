FAQ

Quick answers to common questions Houston businesses ask when planning a new website.

How much does web design cost in Houston? +
Pricing depends on scope (pages, custom design, integrations, copy, and SEO needs). If you share your goals and examples you like, we can send a clear estimate and timeline.
How long does a website take to build? +
Timelines vary by scope. Smaller sites are typically faster; larger builds with custom sections, motion, copywriting, and SEO structure take longer. After a short discovery, we provide a realistic timeline.
Do you redesign existing websites? +
Yes. Website redesign is one of the best ways to improve clarity, performance, and conversions— especially if your current site is slow, outdated, or not generating leads. See Website Redesign.
Do you help with SEO? +
Yes. We build SEO-ready structure (headings, internal linking, speed, semantic markup) and can support ongoing SEO depending on your goals.
What platforms do you build on? +
We can build custom-coded sites and also work with modern platforms depending on what fits your business, editing needs, and performance goals.
